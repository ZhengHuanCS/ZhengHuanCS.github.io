Biography

I am a master student at school of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT), and Shenzhen Research Institute of Big data, Shenzhen, China. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Associate at the School of Operations Research and Information Engineering, Cornell University, Ithaca, USA. I completed my PhD at City University of Hong Kong in Electronic Engineering, 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Tommy W.S. Chow (FIEEE). And during my PhD, I was a visiting scholar at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Wisconsin-Madison, USA. I was a Research Assistant at The University of Hong Kong from 2013 to 2015.
