Biography

I am a Research Assistant Professor at the School of Data Science, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), and Shenzhen Research Institute of Big data, Shenzhen, China. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Associate at the School of Operations Research and Information Engineering, Cornell University, Ithaca, USA. I completed my PhD at City University of Hong Kong in Electronic Engineering, 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Tommy W.S. Chow (FIEEE). And during my PhD, I was a visiting scholar at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Wisconsin-Madison, USA. I was a Research Assistant at The University of Hong Kong from 2013 to 2015.

I am looking for PhD students, Research Assistants, and Visiting Students. If you are interested in these positions, please send me your CV/resume.

Research Interests

My research interests include machine learning, computer vision, and optimization. I also worked on statistical process control and neuroscience signal/data processing. My research projects relate to:

  • low-rank/high-rank matrix factorization/recovery/completion
  • recommendation system
  • automatic machine learning
  • clustering algorithms
  • tensor decomposition/recovery
  • non-smooth optimization
  • deep learning
  • process monitoring, fault detection, and diagnosis
  • EEG and fMRI signal processing
